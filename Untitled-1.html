Top 3 Destinations in Vietnam

Ha Long Bay

Ha Long Bay is known for its emerald waters and thousands of towering limestone islands topped with rainforests.

Hoi An Ancient Town

Hoi An is a well-preserved example of a Southeast Asian trading port dating from the 15th to the 19th century.

Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park

Phong Nha-Ke Bang is famous for its cave systems including some of the largest and most beautiful in the world.

  • Rich cultural heritage
  • Delicious traditional food
  • Friendly and welcoming people
